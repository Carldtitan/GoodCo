# Product

## Register

product

## Users

GoodCo is used by Bay Area pantry staff, volunteers, and network admins who are receiving food, checking stock, and coordinating pantry-to-pantry transfers during operating hours.

## Product Purpose

GoodCo turns real receiving events into reviewed inventory lots, then exposes eligible lots to a governed local marketplace. Success means pantry staff can enter inventory quickly, trust what is available, and avoid invented marketplace activity.

## Brand Personality

Calm, practical, accountable.

## Anti-references

GoodCo must not feel like a marketing landing page, a chatbot wrapper, a decorative dashboard, or a consumer shopping app. Avoid explanatory blocks, oversized hero sections, fake activity, and preloaded inventory.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the task.
- Use real records only.
- Make confidence and review status visible.
- Keep controls familiar.
- Prefer short labels over instruction copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, clear focus states, reduced-motion support, and plain language for busy volunteers.
